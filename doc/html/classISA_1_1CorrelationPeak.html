Detailed Description

Maintains quantities associated with single correlation peak.

This consists of the image point on the comparison image that corresponds to the feature in the comparison image, and the strength (confidence level) of the correlation (0.0 - 1.0). There will be more than one peak (one instance of this class) per REF image feature maintained by the CorrelationTiePoint. The CTP will decide, based on correlation strength and consistency with neighboring CTPs, whether a peak should be considered as the correct correlation point.

Wish List: May eventually want to consider a 2x2 (2-D) correlation covariance matrix to accompany the peak value.
